CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
IMAGE NAME DESCRIPTION
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin

  • This young man is portrayed as one of the prosperous Filipinos who was successful in finishing his education abroad.

  • Maria Clara's love interest.

Pilosopo Tasyo

  • Pilosopo Tasyo is a representation of the well-educated Filipinos who once admired Spanish colonial culture.

  • He is known as Tasyong baliw due to his knowledge.

Padre Salvi

  • He is the friar who took control over San Diego after Padre Damaso.

  • He does not physically hurt the people when punished, but he asks for charge.

Maria Clara

  • The love interest of Crisostomo Ibarra.

  • The character of aria Clara is portrayed as a dependable young lady who is a devoted friend and obedient daughter.

Padre Damaso

  • Also known as Padre Garote due to his brutal personality who punishes the Filipinos with physical abuse.

  • The biological father of Maria Clara.

Alferez

  • His personality is known to be as bad as a devil.

  • He considers his marriage to be an unlucky event of his life.
